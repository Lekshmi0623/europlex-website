How We Make It

Advanced HDPE Pipe
Manufacturing Process

A vertically integrated production process ensures consistent quality from raw material to finished product.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

We source only certified PE 80, PE 100, and PE 100-RC compounds from approved resin manufacturers, with every batch tested for MFI, density, and carbon-black content before entering production.

  • Exclusive use of virgin-grade PE compounds
  • 100% traceability from resin lot to finished pipe
  • In-house compound testing laboratory
01

Raw Material Inspection

Incoming resin tested against ISO and EN standards for melt flow, density, and contamination.

02

Extrusion & Forming

Computer-controlled extrusion lines maintain dimensional tolerances to ±0.1 mm across all diameters.

03

In-Line Quality Control

Continuous laser measurement, wall-thickness scanning, and pressure testing during production.

04

Final Testing & Certification

Hydrostatic pressure tests, dimensional checks, and third-party certification before dispatch.
